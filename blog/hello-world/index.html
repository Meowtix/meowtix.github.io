Hello World!

Welcome to my blog. I would like to use this opportunity to introduce myself.
I am Matic, a web developer with wide range of experience, at the moment I am focusing on front-end.
I like making pretty and easy to use websites.

So, what can you expect from this blog?

I decided that I am going to share stories, thoughts and knowledge I’ve gathered during the years.
Not only that, I will share the struggles of learning how to code, and trying to better myself.

I have been a Linux and Unix enthusiast since my early teens. I am a very curious individual. You can definitely expect me talking about Linux and Unix.
I like to try out different distros, customize them to my liking and in general fiddle with them.

What drives me?

I am in constant pursuit of knowledge, basically I know that by bettering myself I can achieve my goals.
I fell in love with computers at an early age, but haven’t touched code way later when I was doing an internship as a sysadmin.

I got a task of writing some bash and Python scripts. I struggled a lot, but I managed to make them work with some help.
This was the defining moment, where I decided that I actually like writing code. A couple of years of me building Android apps and making websites I got my first web developer job.

I consider myself lucky, because I still feel like I don’t know enough, yet people pay me to write code.
It means a lot, that’s why I am trying to fill in some gaps and gain knowledge.

Conclusion

Expect to see more content as I find the time to write. Linux, algorithms, coding challenges and struggles will pop up in the blog as I gather my thoughts and write them down.

Happy coding!
